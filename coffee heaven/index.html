Fun Facts About Coffee

  • Coffee is the second most traded commodity in the world.
  • There are over 100 species of coffee plants.
  • Espresso has less caffeine than brewed coffee.
  • Decaf coffee is not completely caffeine-free.
  • Finland consumes the most coffee per capita.
